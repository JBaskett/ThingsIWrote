About Me

I believe that words are powerful. I also believe that words can and should be fun. Sometimes I write with purpose but much of the time I just like to style satisfying sentences with varying degrees of sense! While it is fun to write, words should be spoken. They have rhythm, they rhyme; we give them tone and pitch. Words should be spoken. Words should also be shared and that’s why I’ve created this site!

I also believe that The Word (God’s creative power behind the universe) became flesh and walked among us. Words are powerful and God's word is especially powerful! 

In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God. He was in the beginning with God. All things were made through Him, and without Him nothing was made that was made… The Word became flesh and dwelt among us, and we beheld His glory, the glory as of the only begotten of the Father, full of grace and truth.

So that's a bit about why I write. Check out the poems and the blog to see what I write and most of all enjoy being here!

James Baskett
