Workshops

Without words we cannot even say nothing, with words, we can if we choose, say nothing at all.

With so much of our lives defined by words and so many of our interactions dependent upon them, language holds a central role in each of our lives. Sadly many people are daunted by language and have not discovered the wonder of self expression and the joy to be found in being creative. While I am happy to tailer a session to any ability, my workshops are designed to encourage people to pick up a pen and get their poems from that pen to the page.

Below you will find the elements of a workshop package designed for schools. I can tailor workshops to any setting. If you are considering booking a workshop, please don’t hesitate to contact me to discuss your requirements.

 

A package may include any of the following:

  • All age Intro
    • Interactive session including the performance of selected poems and the exploration of rhythm in language to capture the students imaginations and inspire creativity with words.
  • Workshops
    • A series of activities designed to get poems from the pen to the page by getting creative and exploring how we can have fun with words as a class
  • A Writers Club
    • An opportunity for more advanced writers to hone their skills
  • Poetry Open Mic (lunchtime)
    • Informal performances of selected poems and pocket poems created in Workshops
  • The Grand Finale
    • An opportunity for students to perform some of their work or poems they have discovered to the whole school or year group at the end of the day.
  • Book Signing
    • I have copies of my poetry collection and my finish the poem work book available for pre-order so that the creativity needn’t stop at the end of the day. These can be collected and I am willing to sign books so that students can take away a personalised book to inspire them to keep on writing!

For more details on any of the above, please don’t hesitate to contact me.

 

 

James Baskett
